About Me

Being of service to others is what brings true happines. -Marie Osmond


I am a graduate of University of Central Florida. Though Florida has always been my home at heart, for several years I lived outside of the continental U.S. I capitalized this opportunity by spending a lot of time traveling. While living in Germany, a worked as a Jr. Project Manager for the D.O.D. Housing Office.

Germany, my family moved to Anchorage, Alaska. There I leveraged my superior customer service skills to clam and assist frustrated clients on behalf of Chugach Electric Association. Here in Orlando, FL I utilized my B.A. degree in Business Administration, 3.3 G.P.A., while working as a Quality Administrator for Ravago Americas. During this time I created a training program for a newly implemented task management system that remains in use today.

Recently, I completed a Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp program designed by Trilogy Education Services and backed by UCF.
